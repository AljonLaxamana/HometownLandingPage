City of the Philippines

Eplore the wonders of Tarlac

Tarlac, officially the Province of Tarlac (Kapampangan: Lalawigan ning Thvarlac; Pangasinan: Luyag na Tarlac; Ilocano: Probinsia ti Tarlac; Tagalog: Lalawigan ng Tarlac; [tɐɾˈlɐk]), is a landlocked province in the Philippines located in the Central Luzon region. Its capital is the city of Tarlac. It is bounded on the north by the province of Pangasinan, Nueva Ecija on the east, Zambales on the west and Pampanga in the south. The province comprises three congressional districts and is subdivided into 17 municipalities and one city, Tarlac City, which is the provincial capital.

Places to go in Tarlac Philippines

San Jose, Tarlac, Luzon, Philippines
If you want to experience serenity and at the same time commune with the Almighty, this is an excellent place. The most wonderful feature is the chapel sanctuary of the relic of the cross. You will be awed by the story of the monastery and how the relic got there...
ISDAAN VILLAGE
Gerona, Tarlac, Luzon, Philippines
Always an excellent dining experience with friends here. Large portions of well prepared pork and seafood dishes available combined with good service. Interesting "floating" atmosphere with bamboo flooring over the water below and walkways connecting the dining areas allowing water views. Very enjoyable and highly recommended...
CANDING FALLS
Camiling, Tarlac, Luzon, Philippines
It is well worth the trip to not only see these falls but also swim beneath them and for the brave jump from them...
